About Me

I am Max Grier and I am from a town outside of Seattle Washingont called Gig Harbor. I grew up playing sports, camping, boating, and enjoying the Pacific Nortwest.

I currently work as a Financial Analyst at The Boeing Company. I started our in Product Development supporting Technology Integration and Advanced Production Systems, but have recently transitioned to a role supporting the Director of Flight Sciences. In this role, I have found coding can significantly increase efficiencies. By learning some VBA, I have been able to automate many of my manual reports which gives me more time to analyze the data.

As I continue to learn ways to automate reporting through VBA and Python, my interest in Computer Science grows. There are so many improvements businesses can make with automation while also reducing chance for human error. I would like continue down the path of Data Analytics to drive well informed business decisions.
